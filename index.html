Cards With Friends!

Created by: John Damits

Version: 1.5.0 Updated: 12/2/20

Objective: Have fun with friends over an online card table!

Controls / Info:

  • Click and drag cards to move them.
  • Double Click to reveal card to everyone (the card will flip RIGHT).
  • Right Click or Tap to peek card (only you can see, the card will flip UP with an outline).
  • Cards with an outline are currently being peeked by another player (outline color corresponds to player color).
  • Double Click eraser tool to clear all drawings.
  • Drag chips to the chip split / combine area and click button to make change.
  • Drag your nametag from underneath the "Deal" button to stake your claim on the table.

New Features:

  • Tanks! (WASD to move tank, Arrow Keys to move gun, spacebar to shoot your friends)
  • Chip splitter and combiner: to have less one dollar chips at the end of a poker round
  • Color picker: to make choosing your favorite color easier
